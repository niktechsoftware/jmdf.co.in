Placement Fair

Every year, placement fair is organized by the institution where all the premium companies or employers visit RBMI Business School for recruitment. The institute boasts of a huge list of employers who have witnessed the performance of the alumni and have also seen a significant rise in first time recruiters.

Placements are benchmark to the performance of any management institute and it depicts the success and growth of the institutions. RBMI Universe is proud of all the achievements that have been made particularly in this field.

RBMI 11th CONSECUTIVE PLACEMENT FAIR – 2016

RBMI organized its 11th Placement fair on 7th of May, 2016. This season saw a healthy mix of companies from different sectors such as E-Commerce, Manufacturing, Pharmaceuticals recruiting for their sales and marketing functions. Companies offered pre-placement interviews to the students based on their outstanding performance. 42 companies offered 510 jobs at RBMI campus.

In this placement fair 2100 students from Dehradun, Haldwani, Moradabad, Rampur, Saharanpur, Meerut, Ghaziabad, Noida, Shahjahanpur, Sitapur, Lakhimpur, Lucknow, Banaras, Jaunpur etc. have participated. 510 students got selected from these cities and majority of students got selected from Rakshpal Bahadur Group of Institutions.

Executive Chairperson of RBMI Mrs. Veena Mathur, Director – Dr. Neeraj Saxena, Managing Director Er. Naveen Prasad Mathur gave their blessings to the selected students and motivated the other students to make their better efforts in next placement fair.

RBMI reinforced its position as one of the premier college in northern India as it successfully completed its placement fair once again in the year 2016.

Placement Fair – 2015

At a time jobs are becoming scarce and pink slips the order of the day with the global meltdown, there is reason to cheer. Rakshpal Bahadur Group of Institutions organized its tenth consecutive Placement Fair, dated May 9, 2015. The main endeavor of Rakshpal Bahadur Management Institute, Bareilly is to get students placed in reputed national and multinational companies. Rakshpal Bahadur Management Institute assures logistic support to the visiting companies at every stage of the Placement process by making Institute infrastructure available to them. The Institute acts as an interface between the industry and the students, and primarily enables the students to select from their career options. The Institute facilitates the selection process of all the companies as per their requirement.

This year more than 2380 students had registered for the placement drive. In all representatives from 41 national and multinational companies recruited 450 candidates.

The selected candidates were form MBA, MCA, Engineering, and Pharmacy courses of Rakshpal Bahadur Group of Institutions. The Executive Chairperson Mrs. Veena Mathur, Managing Director Er. Navin Prasad Mathur and Director Dr. Neeraj Saxena wished the selected students for their bright career.

The recruiting companies included VMC, Addeco, Genpact, Haldiram, Technosys, HCL, Idea, IGL, Lobo Pharma, Lupin, Mission Research, Muthoot Group, Spectrum, Spice, Tata Capital, Adhay, Airtel, Appin Technologies, Aromatic, Assured Job, Teleperformance, Policy Bazar, Call to Connect, Camphor & Allied, Coca Cola, Cosmic, Delhivery, Emcure Pharma, Exide, UVIMAC, Varroc, Aurangabad Auto mobiles, Data Transmission, Vadilal, Vgaurd, Vodafone, Wockhardt Pharma, Alozan etc. Some companies interviewed the students in several rounds and their results were declared in the evening. The salary packages offered were up to 4.30 L per annum.

Students from Dehradun, Haridwar, Haldwani, Moradabad, Rampur, Saharanpur, Meerut, Ghaziabad, Noida, Shahjahanpur, Sitapur, Lakhimpur-Khiri, Lucknow, Varanasi, Jaunpur and other small towns visited the Fair whereas the recruited students were jubilant at their success, while the others expressed satisfaction at the thought that they could at least identify their shortcomings after being interviewed. They resolved to eliminate their flaws, and to improve upon their performance.

The Institute authorities were delighted with the event. There has been overwhelming response from the students, who also felt encouraged after getting a good job. Students were happy with the opportunities offered to them in the Placement Fair.

All the faculty members of Business management, Engineering, IT, Pharmacy and B.Ed worked relentlessly for providing necessary help to students. In order to make the event a success, Director Dr. Neeraj Saxena, Director (Engineering) Dr. Vineet Agarwal, Director (Administration) Dr. Ashish Misra, Director (IT) Dr. Rajesh Verma, Associate Director (Pharmacy) Dr. Vikas Saxena, HOD (B.Ed) Dr. Amita Bhatnagar and the Head of corporate resource center Anoop Saxena, Mrs. Anshika Prakash and the administrative staff made a commendable contribution.

Placement Fair- 2013

RBMI Group of Institutions has organized Eighth Consecutive Placement Fair on 04th May, 2013. In this fair more than 2800 students from different Institutes have participated. Students of RBMI group and other colleges got placement in different Multinational and National companies. The Placement Fair was inaugurated by Executive Chairperson Ms. Veena Mathur along with Managing Director Mr. Naveen Mathur. Group Director, Dr. Neeraj Saxena welcomed all recruiters of different companies as well as all the candidates.

FAIR Highlights: More than 50 companies from Delhi, NCR, Indore, Lucknow, Rudrapur, Chennai and Uttarakhand graced the occasion with their presence. In the fair, companies from almost all sectors like FMCG, Manufacturing, Real Estate, Pharmaceuticals, Automobile, Software Development, Insurance, BPO, KPO, Telecommunications, Electronics, Consultancy Services, etc joined hand in hand to select the students in different work areas as per their requirements. The selection process adopted was Group Discussion, Personal Interviews and Presentations to check technical know-how and competencies of the candidates. Students from Bareilly, Lucknow, Pune, Varanasi, Shahjahnpur, Mathura, Agra, Rampur, Moradabad, Badaun, Pilibhit etc participated in this event. The Placement Fair reflected the Social Responsibility of Rakshpal Bhadur Management Institution towards the Rohilkhand Region to provide an opportunity where all students could get a fair chance to participate and get a job. According to participating candidates this fair organized by RBMI provided them with varieties of companies to grab a job of their choice. Selected candidates were offered Salary Packages up to 4.80 lacs. Some of the candidates were lucky enough to bag multiple offers.

Companies Highlights: In the fair companies like Genpact, Apollo Hospitals, Dr. Morepan, Phyzer, Reality Mart, Top Ramen, Cogent, Idea Cellular, Vodafone, Da Milano, Wimco, JK Sugar, Bajaj Allianz, Kesar, Lupin, Sharekhan, Cetpa, Technosys, Adecco, L’Soul, Kotak, Voivo, Head Infosystem etc. selected candidates from MBA, BTech., BPharma, BBA & BCA for different profiles. In the end, offer letters were given by Executive Chairperson Ms. Veena Mathur. Candidates were highly delighted after receiving offer letters and paid their sincere gratitude towards the management of RBMI. Mr. Y K Gupta, Mr. Anoop Saxena and Ms Dilpreet Kaur of Corporate Resource Centre congratulated all the selected candidates. Dr. Vineet Agarwal, Dr. Rajesh Verma, Dr. Ashish Mishra and Dr. Mili Sehgal wished good luck to all selected candidates in their future endeavors. Dr. Manish Agarwal, Dr. Vikas Saxena, Mr. Amit Gupta, Mr. Anupam Saxena and other members of faculty and staff put their hard work to make the event a grant success.
