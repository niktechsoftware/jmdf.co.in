Master in Pharmacy

Course Overview

Rakshpal Bahadur College of Pharmacy (RBCP), Bareilly will strive hard to become a centre of excellence in Pharmaceutical Education and Research and will be national leader in the field of pharmaceutical sciences and pharmacy practice with the mission of strengthening the healthcare of the country.

Master of Pharmacy (M. Pharm) is to contribute and fulfill the national need of specialized trained pharmaceutical man-power through prescribed teaching and training programme. Through this programme of post graduate in pharmacy the students will be educated, trained and inculcated to take research & development responsibilities of the country in large and of the world as whole.

Specialization

We are conducting M. Pharm in two stream:

  • Pharmaceutics
  • Pharmaceutical Chemistry

The programme provides the professionals with sufficient knowledge and skills to understand the concept and design of various pharmaceutical dosage forms. The professional also having ability to develop and evaluate various novel drug delivery systems to fulfill the changing disease and patient’s need. The trained post graduates are very well prepared with knowledge of modern analytical techniques for research in formulation development.

  • Modern Analytical Technique
  • Pharmaceutical Biostatistics & Computer Application
  • Drug Regulatory Affairs & Intellectual Property Rights
  • Product Development
  • Pharm Biotechnology

  • Product Development Practical

Semester-II

  • Pharmaceutical Production Management
  • Recent Advances in Drug Delivery System
  • Bio pharmaceutics & Pharmacokinetics
  • Synopsis of the proposed dissertation
  •  
  • Practical Day to Day Evaluation
  • Recent Advances in Drug Delivery System Practical
  • Bio pharmaceutics & Pharmacokinetics Practical

Semester-III & IV

  • Dissertation

Medicinal chemistry and pharmaceutical chemistry are disciplines at the intersection of chemistry, especially synthetic organic chemistry, and pharmacology and various other biological specialties, where they are involved with design, chemical synthesis and development for market of pharmaceutical agents, or bio-active molecules (drugs).
